aloha

genuinely pleased to meet ya,

I learned the word aloha growing up on Oʻahu and as a graphic designer, I aim to carry aloha into every meeting; into every project; into every office.

In Hawaiʻi, aloha is a warm smile from a stranger; it is throwing up a shaka when someone lets you into their lane; it is taking care of the beautiful land that takes care of us. As said by Aunty Pilahi Paki, aloha is the essence of relationships in which each person is important to every other person for collective existence. I aspire to infuse my work with sincerity, passion, and the essence of aloha, recognizing that behind every design challenge also lies an opportunity to connect on a human level.

Looking forward to connecting with you!
