Good Pen

A conceptualized greeting card company dedicated to raising awareness of endangered animals and environmental conservation efforts.

The Challenge

The project tasked us with developing a fully realized company, complete with a comprehensive vision that included brand strategy, product ideation, and production.

The Solution

I envisioned a stationary company that not only crafted captivating greeting cards but also served as a platform for raising awareness about endangered animals. However, I encountered challenges in distinguishing their greeting cards from others and in striking the right balance with educational content. Ultimately, I settled on a trifold card design, which showcased the endangered animal's habitat, presented a list of intriguing facts, and featured a space for personalized greetings.

LOGO BRAINSTORM

I studied endangered animals and their challenges. While I initially considered a simple typographic logo, I later saw the potential for something more memorable. Inspired by mail carriers' pouches and envelopes, and driven by the company's wildlife conservation mission, I focused on endangered marsupials. This led to the final logo, blending imagery of a sugar glider with an envelope.

CARD BRAINSTORM

Before settling on tri-fold die-cut cards, I experimented with cards featuring typography and vector art. I decided it wasn't interesting enough.

PACKAGING

I aimed to craft visually striking packaging, incorporating artwork derived from the cards themselves. Through meticulous measurement and test printing, I explored various packaging die-cut designs until I honed in on the perfect configuration for an ideal box, tailored to accommodate both the cards and envelopes.

FINAL

I was content with the end result. The packaging and greeting cards were not only visually engaging but also structured in a unique manner, setting them apart from traditional greeting card sets. Additionally, the cards contributed to raising awareness about endangered animals. I believe these products successfully realized the brand's vision.
