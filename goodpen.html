Good Pen Logo

A logo for a greeting card company dedicated to raising awareness about endangered animals and environmental conservation efforts.

The Challenge

The project was to craft a timeless logo for a company that donates proceeds to wildlife and environmental conservation.

The Solution

In this project, I researched endangered animals and their challenges. Initially considering a basic typographic logo, I later realized the potential for something more memorable. Drawing from the pouches used by mail carriers and envelopes, and driven by the company's wildlife conservation mission, I explored endangered marsupials. These pouch-bearing animals inspired the final logo, blending the imagery of a sugar glider with that of an envelope.

BRAINSTORM

In my usual creative process, I start off with an extensive word-map. However, this concept struck me early on. I quickly drew a connection between greeting cards and marsupials, given their shared feature of pouches. It struck me that just as mail carriers use pouches to carry letters, envelopes also function as pouches.

FINAL

I experimented with various typefaces. I chose a robust serif for the main wordmark, and for the secondary wordmark, I incorporated a touch of playfulness. The use of serif typefaces evoked a sense of quality "paper goods" to me, much like ones found in a good hardcover book.
