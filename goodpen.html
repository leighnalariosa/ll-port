Good Pen Logo

A logo for a greeting card company dedicated to raising awareness about endangered animals and environmental conservation efforts.

The Challenge

The project was to craft a timeless logo for a company that donates proceeds to wildlife and environmental conservation.

The Solution

In this project, I studied endangered animals and their challenges. While I initially considered a simple typographic logo, I later saw the potential for something more memorable. Inspired by mail carriers' pouches and envelopes, and driven by the company's wildlife conservation mission, I focused on endangered marsupials. This led to the final logo, blending imagery of a sugar glider with an envelope.

BRAINSTORM

In my usual creative process, I start off with an extensive word-map. However, this concept struck me early on. I quickly drew a connection between greeting cards, mail carriers, and marsupials, given their shared feature of pouches.

FINAL

I experimented with various typefaces. I chose a robust serif for the main wordmark, and for the secondary wordmark, I incorporated a touch of playfulness. The use of serif typefaces evoked a sense of quality "paper goods" to me, much like ones found in a good hardcover book.
