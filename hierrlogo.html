HIERR Project Logo

A logo for an organization aiming to foster connections between the private/ public sectors and the community, with the goal of shaping a better Hawaiʻi.

The Challenge

The client requested a logo to enhance their recognition and credibility within the community. This federally-funded project strived to work collaboratively with Hawaiʻi's government sectors, residents, businesses, and service-based organizations to develop a coordinated statewide plan that addressed, reduced, and mitigated the impacts of an economic disaster, as well as make long-term recommendations for Hawaiʻi's economic resilience.

The Solution

This project required research into Hawaiʻi's rich history. My goal was to select a symbol that captured the diverse demographic and intricate history of Hawaiʻi while maintaining cultural sensitivity. I ran into many challenges of picking a symbol. Some options leaned too heavily on representing a certain demographic or meaning, while other options seemed to be too vague or cliche. Striking a balance for the logo, which encompassed Hawaiʻi's diverse demographic and the organization's mission and values, turned this into one of my most challenging projects.

ITERATIONS

I initially presented three logos and they decided on one. After I worked in color for the final logo, they realized the logo did not work. I began the brainstorm process again by word-mapping, sketching, and getting into Illustrator. This is just a snapshot of my brainstorming process.

STYLE TILES

I curated three style tiles but ultimately chose "Citrus Pop" because of how the vibrant colors created contrast and brought energy and playfulness to the brand identity.

CUSTOM ILLUSTRATIONS

I went through many rounds of designing icons. I received feedback that some were too intricate or detailed. Others were difficult to identify. I landed on icons that I thought were vibrant, simple, and iconic.

FUNCTIONALITY

I aimed to distinguish the features of this app from others by implementing unique elements. For instance, I envisioned recipe videos being presented in an engaging manner by incorporating playing videos. Furthermore, I devoted time to brainstorming a clever logo design, aiming to add an extra touch of creativity.
